Nabd Al-Gharb Desktop v1.6.8 - Mini Store Price Visibility Permissions

Changes:
1. Admin can control price visibility separately for each Mini Store.
2. Available permissions: Mini Store Price, Retail Price, Wholesale Price, and Stock Value.
3. Assigned cashier sees only the price fields enabled by Admin.
4. Admin always sees all price columns and stock value.
5. Retail Price support added to Mini Store inventory.
6. Permissions are saved under nag_mini_store_price_permissions_v1 and included in backup/cloud sync keys.
7. Existing Vehicle Stock, Mini Store inventory, invoice, and auto-update configuration remain preserved.
